    This website talks about the British television show Doctor Who.

    OVERVIEW

    Doctor Who is a British television show about a time lord, who is called the Doctor. The Doctor is from a different planet called Gallifrey. The doctor travels through time using a time machine called the TARDIS.

    DOCTOR WHO?

    The Doctor is a time lord from the alien planet, Gallifrey. Time lords do not die, but can regenerate and each time they do they gain a new appearance. Throughout the seasons of the television show the Doctor regenerates several times.

    THE TARDIS

    The TARDIS is a time machine, and stands for Time and Relative Dimensions in Space.
